# PONG 2024

**PONG 2024** is a simple implementation of the classic Pong game developed using the **raylib** library in C++. The game features a bouncy ball, two paddles, and a scoring system, providing an enjoyable gaming experience.

## Features

- **Bouncy Ball**: The game includes a bouncy ball that moves around the screen, bouncing off the paddles and walls.
- **Paddles**: Two paddles are present on the left and right sides of the screen, controlled by the players. The paddles deflect the ball when it collides with them.
- **Scoring System**: A scoring system keeps track of each player's score, incrementing when the opponent misses the ball.
- **Collision Detection**: The game incorporates collision detection to detect when the ball collides with the paddles or walls, allowing for realistic gameplay.

## How to Play

1. **Controls**:
   - **Left Player**: Use the `W` and `S` keys to move the left paddle up and down, respectively.
   - **Right Player**: Use the `Up Arrow` and `Down Arrow` keys to move the right paddle up and down, respectively.

2. **Objective**:
   - The objective of the game is to score points by making the opponent miss the ball.
   - Each time the opponent misses the ball, a point is awarded to the opposing player.

3. **Gameplay**:
   - Move your paddle up and down to deflect the ball and prevent it from passing your side of the screen.
   - Be quick and precise in your movements to outmaneuver your opponent and score points.

4. **Scoring**:
   - Points are awarded to the opposing player each time the ball passes beyond their paddle and touches the wall behind them.
